### 🖼️ 이미지 프롬프트 6가지 (권력 역전 시퀀스)

**Scene 1: 현실의 시작 (허름함과 무시)**
*   **테마:** 현실의 어둠 (The Harsh Reality)
*   **프롬프트:** A hyper-realistic, cinematic shot. A young man with tired, worn-out features, wearing a slightly stained, faded work vest, standing in a dimly lit, fluorescent-lit convenience store aisle at night. Focus on the texture of the worn clothing and the harsh, cold fluorescent light reflecting off his tired face. Cinematic lighting, deep shadows, gritty texture, 8K resolution. *Contrast focus: Utter poverty vs. mundane setting.*

**Scene 2: 권위의 시도 (절박함과 갈망)**
*   **테마:** 현실의 갈망 (Desperation Seeking Help)
*   **프롬프트:** A close-up, low-angle shot. The same young man, now looking desperate and pleading, standing nervously outside a sleek, imposing modern office building at dusk. The light source is the cold blue glow of a phone screen illuminating his anxious face. Emphasize the contrast between his worn appearance and the imposing, cold architecture behind him. Dramatic, high contrast, cinematic depth of field. *Contrast focus: Humble plea vs. Corporate ambition.*

**Scene 3: 인식의 절정 (최고급 공간)**
*   **테마:** 인식의 빛 (The Apex of Perception)
*   **프롬프트:** A breathtaking, wide-angle shot. An opulent, dimly lit, high-end hotel VIP lounge with dark mahogany wood and velvet seating. A sharply dressed, impeccably tailored man in a bespoke suit is entering the frame, radiating silent, undeniable power. The lighting is warm, rich, and luxurious, casting long shadows. Focus on the atmosphere of exclusive wealth. *Contrast focus: Opulence vs. Underlying tension.*

**Scene 4: 충격의 순간 (정체성의 노출)**
*   **테마:** 극적인 대비 (The Moment of Shock)
*   **프롬프트:** An intense, medium close-up shot capturing a moment of frozen shock. The face of the powerful investor, illuminated by the soft, warm light of the VIP lounge, staring directly at the previously described convenience store worker, whose expression is a mixture of surprise and dawning realization. The background should be blurred, emphasizing the direct, intense eye contact and the sudden collision of worlds. High dynamic range (HDR), intense emotional tension. *Contrast focus: Power vs. Identity.*

**Scene 5: 권력 역전 (아이러니한 대화)**
*   **테마:** 권력 역전 (Power Reversal Dialogue)
*   **프롬프트:** A visually symbolic scene focusing on the dialogue. The powerful investor, seated in a high-backed leather chair, leaning slightly forward with a knowing, subtle smirk. The convenience store worker is seated opposite him, looking utterly bewildered but holding a flicker of unexpected self-possession. Use deep, rich colors for the investor and muted, slightly washed-out tones for the worker to emphasize the shift in status. Focus on the subtle shift in body language. *Contrast focus: Dominance vs. Unexpected realization.*

**Scene 6: 최종 질문 (관객 참여 유도)**
*   **테마:** 공감과 질문 (Empathy and Question)
*   **프롬프트:** A highly conceptual, symbolic image. A split screen effect. On the left side, a blurry, dark image representing 'financial ruin' (broken ledger, shadows). On the right side, a sharply focused, bright image representing 'the choice' (a single spotlight shining on a path forward, or a question mark superimposed over a glittering skyline). The overall mood is intense, challenging the viewer. Cinematic composition, strong visual dichotomy. *Contrast focus: Despair vs. Opportunity.*
